Street
Artw

In early 2015, a window of opportunity came along for me to step outside the life I had in New York City and do something I had never done before: travel outside of North America. (Thanks, Walter Mitty, and Jose Gonzales!) The thought was both terrifying and exhilarating. 3 months later, after recruiting a friend to travel with, focused budgeting and some clever planning, we met in Austin, TX to wrap up any last details in person before setting off.

Around Austin, I started taking photos of all the murals adorning the walls of abandoned buildings and thriving businesses and everything in between. Street art of any kind has always piqued my interest, especially with my background in graphic design, but I had never taken the time to record what caught my eye. What follows is a slightly obsessive collection of street art that I encountered across Europe, Asia, Oceania, and North America.

N.B.
All images currently link to posts on my Instagram account. I have yet to implement a slideshow view on this site.
